All About the Weta

Habitat & Diet

Wetas live in the brackish "in-between" spaces like mangrove swamps and tidal creeks. They eat snails, crabs, and bivalves.

Life Cycle

Living up to 40 years, these turtles are slow to mature, making every single individual vital for the population's survival.

The Modern Threat

From "Ghost Traps" (abandoned crab pots) to coastal development, human expansion is the Weta's biggest challenge.
